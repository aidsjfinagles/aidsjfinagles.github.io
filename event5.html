Technical Paper Presentation

  • Paper Presentation can be on any specific topic which is relevant to current technologies.

  • Presentations can be done as a Team or an Individual also.

  • Each team can have maximum of 2 members.

  • Each team can take upto maximum of 15 min (10 min presentation).

Welcome to our national-level technical symposium, hosted at JCT College of Engineering and Technology! This event is a platform for students from across the country to showcase their technical prowess, exchange ideas, and engage in insightful discussions. With a diverse range of competitions, our symposium aims to inspire and empower the next generation of innovators and problem solvers. Throughout the symposium, participants will have the opportunity to participate in technical competitions covering areas such as robotics, coding, electronics, and more. These competitions are designed to challenge students' skills and foster creativity, encouraging them to think outside the box and come up with innovative solutions.
